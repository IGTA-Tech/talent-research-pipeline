# O-1A Visa — Extraordinary Ability Criteria Reference

The O-1A visa is for individuals with extraordinary ability in sciences, education, business, or athletics. Applicants must demonstrate sustained national or international acclaim and meet at least **3 of the following 8 criteria**:

## 1. Awards and Prizes
Nationally or internationally recognized prizes or awards for excellence in the field.
- Examples: Nobel Prize, Pulitzer, industry-specific awards, best paper awards, innovation prizes
- Key: Must be for EXCELLENCE, not just participation

## 2. Membership in Associations
Membership in associations that require outstanding achievements as judged by recognized experts.
- Examples: National Academy of Sciences, IEEE Fellow, ACM Fellow, professional boards
- Key: Must have SELECTIVE admission criteria based on achievement

## 3. Published Material About the Person
Published material in professional or major trade publications or major media about the person.
- Examples: Features in NYT, Forbes, Bloomberg, Nature, industry journals
- Key: Must be ABOUT the person, not just mentioning them; must be in recognized publications

## 4. Judging the Work of Others
Evidence of participation as a judge of the work of others in the field.
- Examples: Peer review, conference program committees, grant review panels, award juries
- Key: Must be in the SAME or ALLIED field

## 5. Original Contributions of Major Significance
Evidence of original scientific, scholarly, athletic, or business-related contributions of major significance.
- Examples: Patents, novel algorithms, new methodologies, significant products, breakthrough research
- Key: Must show IMPACT and SIGNIFICANCE, not just novelty

## 6. Scholarly Articles
Evidence of authorship of scholarly articles in professional or major trade publications.
- Examples: Peer-reviewed journal articles, conference papers, book chapters
- Key: h-index, citation count, journal impact factor all strengthen this

## 7. Critical or Essential Capacity
Evidence of employment in a critical or essential capacity for organizations with distinguished reputations.
- Examples: C-suite roles, VP/Director at Fortune 500, founding team of notable startups
- Key: Must be CRITICAL role (not just senior) at DISTINGUISHED organization

## 8. High Salary or Remuneration
Evidence of high salary or other significantly high remuneration in relation to others in the field.
- Examples: Top 10% compensation, significant equity, large contract values
- Key: Must be compared to OTHERS IN THE SAME FIELD, not general population

## Scoring Guidelines
- **Strong (3+ criteria)**: Likely qualifies for O-1A with proper documentation
- **Moderate (2 criteria)**: May qualify with additional evidence or comparable evidence arguments
- **Weak (0-1 criteria)**: Unlikely to qualify without significant additional evidence
